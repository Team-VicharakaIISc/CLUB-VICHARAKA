ABOUT US

The Vicharaka Robotics Club at IISc is a dynamic platform for robotics enthusiasts to innovate, collaborate, and excel. Focused on cutting-edge robotics, the club engages in hands-on projects, workshops, and national and international competitions. Members explore areas like autonomous systems, swarm robotics, and industrial automation while fostering interdisciplinary learning. The club's flagship project, the Vicharaka Rover, features a six-wheel rocker-bogie suspension, designed for challenging terrains. By combining research, innovation, and community engagement, Vicharaka empowers members to push technological boundaries and make a meaningful impact in robotics.
